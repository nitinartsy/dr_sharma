Dr. Gopal Sharma, MCh, MRCS

Dr. Gopal Sharma, MCh, MRCS is a skilled robotic urological surgeon dedicated to helping patients navigate their journey with urological cancers.

With specialized fellowship training in robotic surgery and comprehensive urological training from PGIMER Chandigarh, he offers patients access to the most advanced, minimally invasive treatment options available today.

Understanding that a cancer diagnosis can be overwhelming, Dr. Sharma combines surgical excellence with compassionate, personalized care. His expertise in robot-assisted procedures for kidney, prostate, and bladder cancers means smaller incisions, faster recovery times, and improved outcomes for his patients. Whether performing complex partial nephrectomies or nerve-sparing prostatectomies, his focus remains on achieving the best possible results while preserving quality of life.

If you’re seeking expert care for a urological condition, Dr. Sharma welcomes the opportunity to discuss your options and partner with you in your treatment journey.
