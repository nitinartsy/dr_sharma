HOW ROBOTIC SURGERY WORKS

Precision robotic care
every step of the way

1

Minimally Invasive Access

Using the da Vinci Surgical System, Dr. Sharma makes 4–6 small “keyhole” incisions (8–12mm) instead of large open cuts. The robotic camera and instruments are inserted through these ports for maximum precision and minimal trauma.

2

Nerve Sparing & Organ Preservation

Dr. Sharma’s expertise in nerve-sparing techniques preserves quality of life after surgery. Whether performing partial nephrectomies or prostatectomies, the goal is to remove cancer while protecting nerves and surrounding tissue.

3

Faster Recovery & Better Outcomes

Smaller incisions mean less pain, reduced blood loss, and shorter hospital stays. Most patients return to normal activities within 2–3 weeks. The robotic platform’s 3D magnified view ensures unparalleled precision during every procedure.
